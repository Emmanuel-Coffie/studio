# **App Name**: Aura Folio

## Core Features:

- Project Showcase: Visually appealing display of projects with descriptions, tech stacks, and links to live demos/code repositories.
- Skills Visualization: Interactive display of technical skills and areas of expertise with proficiency levels.
- AI-Powered Portfolio Review Tool: AI tool generates feedback on portfolio presentation and suggests improvements based on industry best practices, design principles, and user experience guidelines.
- About Me Section: A concise and engaging narrative about your background, passions, and career goals.
- Contact Form: Easy-to-use contact form for visitors to get in touch with you directly.
- Blog/Article Section: Dedicated space for sharing thoughts, tutorials, and insights related to your field.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#4B0082) to convey professionalism and sophistication.
- Background color: Very light gray (#F0F0F0) to provide a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Electric Purple (#BF00FF) for interactive elements and call-to-action buttons.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' serif for headlines and shorter blocks of text, lending an artistic feel
- Body font: 'Alegreya' serif for the main content to provide readability.
- Clean and minimalist icons that complement the overall design, using a consistent style.
- Use a grid-based layout for easy navigation and consistent presentation of information.
- Subtle animations to enhance user experience and add visual appeal (e.g., smooth scrolling, hover effects).
